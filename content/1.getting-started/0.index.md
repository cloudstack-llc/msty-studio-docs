[Msty Studio](https://msty.ai){target="\_blank"} is a [privacy-first](https://msty.ai/privacy-first){target="\_blank"} AI platform that empowers you to design and run advanced AI workflows using local and online models, directly from a web or desktop interface. Msty offers seamless integration with hundreds of top-tier models, powerful tools like Personas, Toolbox, and Turnstiles, and fully local data storage, ensuring both sophistication and security in your AI processes.

## Getting Started

Subscribers get access to both Msty Studio Web and Desktop.

::alert{type="info" icon="tabler:info-circle"}
Data does not sync between devices. Data is stored locally on your device. Studio web stores data directly to your browser.
::

### Msty Studio Web

Msty Studio Web is a web-based application that is capable of running the marjority of features that Msty Studio offers. However, to unlock additional features, including Real-Time Data, Toolbox, and Local Models, Studio web will need to connect to [Sidecar](/getting-started/sidecar). Sidecar can either be ran on your same device or on a remote device.

Even though Msty App is a web-based app, data is stored directly on your browser's local storage. This helps us maintain our Privacy-first policy and our commitment to keep your data in your control.
