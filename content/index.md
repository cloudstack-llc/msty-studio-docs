# Welcome to Msty Studio

**Msty Studio** is your comprehensive AI development environment that transforms how you work with AI models. Whether you're building AI applications, experimenting with different models, or creating sophisticated AI workflows, Msty Studio provides the tools and flexibility you need.

## What is Msty Studio?

Msty Studio is a powerful web-based platform that gives you:

- **Multi-Model Support**: Connect to local AI models via Msty App or remote providers like OpenAI, Claude, and Gemini
- **Advanced Tooling**: Built-in toolsets, live contexts, and real-time data integration
- **Flexible Workspaces**: Organize your projects and conversations efficiently
- **Developer-Friendly**: Full control over prompts, system messages, and model configurations
- **Seamless Integration**: Works alongside Msty App for enhanced local AI capabilities
