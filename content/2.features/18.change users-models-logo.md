## Customize Model & Provider Appearance

This feature allows you to personalize your AI models and providers by customizing their names and logos. Whether you want to give your AI a unique identity, make conversations feel more natural, or simply add a missing logo, this guide will walk you through the process.

Lets start without further ado!

![Model Logo Customization](/images/change-provider-model-logo.png)

### Changing a Model's Name and Logo

Follow these steps to customize the name and logo of an individual AI model:

1.  **Access Model Hub:** Navigate to the Model Hub</mark>** section of the application.

2.  **Locate Your Model:** Expand the section for your local AI provider (e.g., **Msty local AI**) to reveal the list of available models (e.g., **Cogito**).

3.  **Edit Model Details:** Click the edit button</mark>** (often represented by a pencil icon) located next to the model you wish to customize.

4.  **Change Model Name:**
    *   In the edit window that appears, locate the field.
    *   Modify the text in this field to your desired new name (e.g., "Bob").
    *   Click the button to save the new model name.

5.  **Update Model Logo:**
    *   To replace the existing logo, click directly on the current model logo within the edit window. This action will open a file explorer window.
    *   **Select Your Image:** Browse your computer to locate and select the image file you wish to use as the new logo.
    *   **Confirm:** Click **Open**. The logo will be *immediately* updated to your selected image.
    
6.  **Refresh View:** To ensure the changes are fully reflected in your conversations or model selection dropdowns, you may need to re-select the model or navigate back to your chat interface.

>Congratulations! You've successfully customized your AI model's name and logo to better suit your preferences.

### Changing a Provider's Logo

The process for customizing a provider's logo (such as for **OpenAI** or the **Msty local AI** provider itself) is very similar to changing a model's logo:

*   Instead of clicking on an individual model's logo, simply hover over and click on the provider's logo within the Model Hub.
*   Follow the same steps as above to select and upload your desired image.

>Yay! You've now personalized your AI provider's appearance as well.

### Changing Your User Avatar

To change your own user avatar within Msty, follow these steps:

1. Click on your current user avatar, typically located in your conversations visible as a small user icon.
2. That will open a dropdowns menu. Consisting of three available options to customize your user profile:
    * Upload image - Click on this option to select and upload a new avatar image from your device. 
    * Reset - Lets you revert back to the default avatar image provided by Msty.
    * Clear - Removes your current avatar image, leaving it blank.
3. Choose the desired option and follow the prompts to complete the change.

>And now you have a personalized user avatar to represent you in Msty!

### Image Requirements

When uploading a new logo, please adhere to the following guidelines:

*   **Supported Formats:** PNG, JPEG, GIF
*   **Maximum File Size:** 1 MB
*   **Recommended Dimensions:** 128x128 pixels for optimal display quality

### Why Customize Your Logos?

Customizing your preffered logo for users, models or providers offers several benefits:

*   **Personify Your AI:** Give it a more personal and relatable feel, making interactions more engaging and enjoyable.
*   **Address Missing Logos:** Easily add logos for users, AI providers or custom models (e.g., those downloaded from Hugging Face) that may not have default imagery.
*   **Enhance User Experience:** Create a visually consistent and enjoyable environment tailored to your preferences.

### Advanced Personalization

To take personalization a step further, consider creating a Persona specifically for your customized model. This allows you to define a unique personality, communication style, and set of characteristics, further enriching your interactions with your newly named and branded AI.
