> Ever wished you could finely tune the very "brain" of your AI? Now you can!
> Msty introduces **Customizable Default Prompts**, giving you the power to shape how your AI understands, searches, summarizes, and even titles your conversations.
> Unlock a new level of control and personalize your AI's core intelligence to perfectly match your unique needs.

## Take Control: Customize Your AI's Default Prompts

This powerful feature allows you to **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">personalize the underlying prompts</mark>** that guide your AI's fundamental operations within Msty. These prompts are crucial as they **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">shape how various AI functions operate</mark>**, including generating conversation titles, building search queries, retrieving knowledge, and summarizing context.

**Important Note:** Any changes you make to these prompts are saved to your **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">device’s global settings</mark>** (master database). This means your custom prompts will apply across **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">all workspaces</mark>** on your device, ensuring a consistent and personalized AI experience wherever you work. These settings are *not* configured on a per-workspace basis.

Here are the key AI features whose prompts you can now edit and customize:

### 1. Context Shield Summary: Precision Summaries, Your Way

By default, the **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Context Shield Summary</mark>** is designed to generate clear, structured summaries, highlighting key points and essential takeaways from your conversations.

With custom prompts, you can now **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">take full control</mark>** over this crucial function. Insert your own prompt to precisely dictate **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">how context should be summarized</mark>**. Want a bulleted list, a single paragraph, or a specific focus on user intent? You decide!

### 2. Knowledge Stack Context: Smarter Knowledge Retrieval

This prompt governs how relevant information from your Knowledge Stack is **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">retrieved and provided to the AI</mark>** to answer your queries. It's essential for feeding your AI the right information at the right time.

By customizing this prompt, you can fine-tune the relevance and depth of the context pulled from your knowledge base, making your AI's responses even more insightful and targeted.

### 3. Knowledge Stack Simple Search: Efficient Information Hunting

This prompt guides your AI in generating **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">simple, direct search queries</mark>** specifically for your Knowledge Stack. It's the engine behind quickly finding straightforward information.

Customizing this prompt allows you to optimize how these basic queries are constructed, leading to more efficient and accurate retrieval of information within your knowledge base.

### 4. Knowledge Stack Structured Search: Advanced Data Mining

For more complex information retrieval, the **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Knowledge Stack Structured Search</mark>** prompt comes into play. It's designed to **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">optimize structured search queries</mark>** for your knowledge base systems.

This prompt gives you granular control over **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">how advanced search queries are built</mark>** and, consequently, **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">how deeply and effectively knowledge is retrieved</mark>** from your structured data. Tailor it for precision and speed!

### 5. Real-Time Data Query Synthesis: Master of Web Searches

The **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Real-Time Data Query Synthesis</mark>** prompt is your AI's gateway to the web, generating **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">effective search queries for real-time web searches</mark>** based on user requests.

With this customizable prompt, you gain ultimate control over **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">how your AI builds and optimizes these web search queries</mark>**, directly influencing the relevance and quality of the real-time data it retrieves. Become the architect of your AI's internet expeditions!

### 6. Title Generation: The Art of Naming Conversations

This prompt is responsible for the snappy, **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">concise titles</mark>** Msty generates for your conversations, helping you quickly understand their content at a glance.

By inserting your own prompt, you can **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">guide the AI's creativity and criteria</mark>** for title generation, ensuring they align perfectly with your organizational or personal preferences.

### 7. YouTube Attachments: Enhanced Video Insights

The **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">YouTube Attachments</mark>** prompt governs how Msty processes and extracts information from YouTube videos, including their transcripts.

Customizing this prompt offers **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">robust control over how your AI searches within YouTube content</mark>** and **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">structures the output</mark>**. Define what details are most important to extract, how they should be presented, and fine-tune your video analysis workflow.

### Why Customize Your Default Prompts?

*   **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Tailor AI Behavior</mark>**: Fine-tune your AI to perfectly match your specific workflows, industry jargon, or personal preferences.
*   **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Boost Efficiency</mark>**: Optimize search queries and summary formats for faster, more relevant results.
*   **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Ensure Consistency</mark>**: Maintain a unified tone and style across all AI interactions, especially important for team environments.
*   **<mark style="background-color: #FDFF99">Unlock New Possibilities</mark>**: Experiment with unique prompting strategies to discover novel ways your AI can assist you.

By harnessing the power of customizable default prompts, you're not just using an AI; you're *designing* its intelligence to work precisely for you.
